Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Luli Lens ("Luli Lens", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Luli Lens may process information you provide or generate while using the app, including scanned product information, ingredient text, app preferences, subscription status, and basic device or usage information needed to operate and improve the app.

Camera and Photos

The app may request access to your camera or photos so you can scan food labels and product information. Camera access is used to support scanning features. We do not use camera access for unrelated purposes.

Product and Scan Data

When you scan or search for a product, the app may process product names, barcodes, ingredient lists, nutrition information, and related analysis results. This information is used to provide product insights, warnings, and recommendations inside the app.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We may receive subscription status information from Apple or our subscription service provider in order to unlock paid features and manage access.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for app functionality, analytics, subscriptions, product lookup, image loading, crash diagnostics, or infrastructure. These services may process limited information as needed to provide their services.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, provide food label analysis, personalize the experience, maintain subscriptions, improve performance, troubleshoot issues, and protect the app from misuse.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Your Choices

You can manage camera and photo permissions in your device settings. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Children

Luli Lens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@luli.app
